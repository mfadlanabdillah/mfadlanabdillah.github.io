About Me

My professional journey began in September 2020 when I joined PT Khatulistiwa Nusantara Indonesia as a Software Developer, where I focused on developing, maintaining, troubleshooting, and debugging web and/or mobile applications until April 2021. Since April 2022, I have continued my career journey at PT Bussan Auto Finance, assuming the role of Software Engineer. Specializing in front-end web development, I am proficient in a range of technologies, with a keen interest in back-end development as well. At my current position, I am responsible for leveraging my expertise to enhance software solutions and contribute to the company's success.
